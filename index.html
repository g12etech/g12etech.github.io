The Laws of Physics are Universal

The Law of Inertia

An object at rest remains at rest or if it is in motion continues to be in motion with a constant speed along a straight line unless acted upon by an external force.

The Law of Acceleration

The acceleration of a body is directly proportional to the magnitude of the net force acting on it, in thesame direction as the net force, and inversely proportional to the mass of the body.

The Law of Interaction

When two bodies interact with one another, the forces they exert on one another are equal in magnitude and opposite in direction.
